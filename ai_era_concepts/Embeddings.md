# Embeddings and Latent Space

## Overview
Embeddings are numerical representations (vectors) of text that capture semantic meaning. Similar concepts are placed closer together in "vector space."

## Why it matters in the AI Era
Embeddings are the bridge between human language and computer-efficient retrieval. They are the core technology behind **Semantic Search** and **RAG**.

---

## Key Principles
1. **Dimensionality:** Each embedding is a long list of numbers representing different "features" of the meaning.
2. **Cosine Similarity:** A mathematical way to measure how "similar" two embeddings are.
3. **Vector Space:** The high-dimensional "map" where all embeddings live.

---

## AI Context
Whenever you need to "search by meaning" rather than just "keywords," you use embeddings and a vector database.
