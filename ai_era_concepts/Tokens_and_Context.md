# Tokens and Context Window

## Overview
Tokens are the fundamental units of text that LLMs process. A "Context Window" is the maximum number of tokens a model can consider at once.

## Why it matters in the AI Era
Tokens directly impact **cost** (billing is usually per-token) and **latency**. The context window limits how much "memory" the model has during a conversation.

---

## Key Principles
1. **Tokenization:** Converting text into numerical IDs (words, sub-words, or characters).
2. **Context Limits:** The "short-term memory" constraint of a model.
3. **Information Density:** How much meaning is packed into a given number of tokens.

---

## AI Context
Optimizing token usage and managing long-form context (e.g., through RAG or context compression) are core skills for AI engineers.
